# Screenshots

Place your app screenshots here for F-Droid.

## Naming Convention

Screenshots should be named sequentially:
- 1.png, 2.png, 3.png, etc.

## Requirements

- **Phone Screenshots**: 1080x1920 or similar aspect ratio
- **Tablet Screenshots**: 1536x2048 or similar aspect ratio
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Max file size: 8MB per screenshot

## Recommended Screenshots

1. Dashboard showing net worth and stats
2. Transactions list
3. Budget tracking with progress bars
4. Reports and charts
5. Account management
6. Categories and tags
7. AI Assistant chat
8. Dark mode view

Add your screenshots to the respective directories:
- `phoneScreenshots/` - For phone screenshots
- `tenInchScreenshots/` - For tablet screenshots
