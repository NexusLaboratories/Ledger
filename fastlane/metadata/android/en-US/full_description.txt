Nexus Ledger is a free and open-source personal finance management app that helps you track your accounts, transactions, budgets, and spending patterns.

<b>Key Features:</b>

• <b>Account Management</b>: Create and manage multiple accounts (checking, savings, credit cards, etc.)
• <b>Transaction Tracking</b>: Add income and expenses with categories and tags
• <b>Budget Monitoring</b>: Set budgets and get notifications when you reach spending thresholds
• <b>Reports & Statistics</b>: Visualize your spending patterns with charts and reports
• <b>Export Report Support</b>: Export transactions to xlsx and repots through pdfs
• <b>AI Assistant</b>: Chat with a financial AI assistant for insights about your spending with BYOM 
• <b>Categories & Tags</b>: Organize transactions with customizable categories and tags
• <b>Data Import/Export</b>: Backup and restore your data in JSON format
• <b>Encrypted Database</b>: Your financial data is protected with SQLCipher encryption
• <b>Dark Mode</b>: Switch between light and dark themes
• <b>Multi-Currency Support</b>: Track accounts in different currencies


<b>Privacy & Security:</b>

• All data is stored locally on your device with encryption
• Optional password protection with biometric unlock
• Your financial data stays private and secure

<b>Free & Open Source:</b>

Nexus Ledger is maintained by volunteers who donate their time and resources. This is a FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) project, which means:
• No ads or tracking
• Community-driven development
• Transparent and auditable code

If you find this app valuable, please consider supporting the project to help keep it free and improving!

<b>Notifications:</b>

• Budget threshold alerts (50%, 80%, 90%)
• Monthly report reminders
• Optional donation reminders to support the project

Take control of your financial journey with Nexus Ledger!
